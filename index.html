What makes Himalayan Salt different from regular salt?

The Himalayan Mountains formed over 200 million years ago, rising over sea salt beds and leaving Himalayan Salt hidden deep beneath its snowy peaks. Our Himalayan Salt kitchen products started their journey within these mountains: carefully extracted, crossing land and sea, and brought to your table for all to enjoy.
